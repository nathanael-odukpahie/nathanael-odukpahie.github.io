Gain more success in your personal and professional life with a consultant, a counsellor and a cheerleader to guide you

2021 Mentorship Programs

Who can be a mentor?

There are several characteristics that a mentor should have in order to be effective in their position as guide or advisor for the mentee. Here are some of the things a good mentor should have:

  • Skills and experience in a field or industry
  • Desire or motivation to share wisdom
  • Commitment to extra demands
  • A positive attitude
  • Good communication skills
  • A love for their job
  • A team player
  • Emotional intelligence
Meet Our Mentors

Why do you need a mentor?

Mentoring can give you an edge that differentiates you from your peers and/or your competition. You may be ready to make a career change or advance in your present career but something is holding you back. Having a mentor can provide you with many advantages. They can help to shorten your learning curve, open your mind to new ideas and possibilities, identify opportunities and advise on how to promote yourself.

“Mentoring is a brain to pick, and ear to listen, and a push in the right direction.” - John Crosby
