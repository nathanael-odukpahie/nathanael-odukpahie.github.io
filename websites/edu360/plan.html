EDU 2020

Why attend EDU 2020

Parents

Whether your target market is new or seasoned parents, edu360 will help to connect your brand, products and services to your potential customers.

Schools

edu360 is an invaluable opportunity to showcase your solutions to school managers and owners as well as discover new partnerships and collaborations.

Students

Are you looking to reach a diverse range of students between the ages of 7 to 19? edu360 is an excellent platform to meet and engage them.

Government

Build valuable connections with representatives from several government parastatals and regulatory agencies within the education sector to collaborate on projects beneficial to the sector.

Teachers

Connect with the people nurturing the next generation of leaders and contribute, in your own way, to the advancement of the teaching profession in Nigeria.

Industry Experts

Connect with thought leaders, influencers and service providers from a wide range of sectors.

This year’s theme: Education beyond walls

Welcome to the 2019 edition of edu360, themed “Education Beyond Walls.” We believe education should be a holistic 360 learning experience that extends well beyond the classroom walls. Learning is not limited to students alone; educators, parents and stakeholders from all walks of life will benefit from the insights and global trends to be featured at this year’s edition. Get ready for activities that will engage all your senses and prepare our future leaders for the realities of 21st Century learning.
