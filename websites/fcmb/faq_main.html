FCMB Business Zone is a resource center for SMEs and Entrepreneurs in Nigeria. It provides access to the right information about funding, business advisory, training services, networking events and innovative technology services.

If you are a STANDARD USER, follow these steps to sign up:

1. Visit https://businesszone.fcmb.com/ signup

2. Complete the form

3. Click ‘Register’

4. You will receive a link in the email address you provided; simply click on the link to activate your account

If you are a BUSINESS OWNER, follow these steps to sign up:

1. Visit https://businesszone.fcmb.com/merchantreg

2. Complete the required fields

3. Click ‘Submit’

4. You will receive a link in the email address you provided; simply click on the link to activate your account
