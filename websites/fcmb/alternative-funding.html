ALTERNATIVE FUNDING

At First city monument bank, we strive to support our customer’s business by providing low interest and competitive funds available for our customer’s working capital, asset finance/ acquisition, business expansion need. The fund can be access by customers that operate in segment like trade, manufacturing, wholesale, health care hospitality and school. We have leverage on our partnership with DBN and BOI to ensure that our customers benefit from this low interest funds.

Our Objectives:

  • To grow MSMEs sector by increasing their productivity and output.
  • Improve access to finance to SMEs through alternative source of funding.
  • Reduce the high financing costs for MSMEs of all sizes when sourcing for funds from commercial banks.

Loan Amount: Minimum N5m and Maximum N200m single obligor

Interest rate: Highly competitive

Moratorium: up to 1 year

Tenor: up to 10 years

Repayment structures: Monthly, Quarterly.
