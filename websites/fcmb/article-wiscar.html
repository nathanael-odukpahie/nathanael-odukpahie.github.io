The SheVentures Mentorship with WISCAR

FCMB held an event to mark the end of a 12-week long Mentorship Programme under its SheVentures proposition for 80 women entrepreneurs.

The SheVentures Mentorship initiative, which was in collaboration with WISCAR, provided female entrepreneurs with an opportunity to connect, network and drive sustainable advancement in their personal and professional lives.

At the event were high profile mentors such as Habiba Balogun - Director & Principal Consultant, Habiba Balogun Consulting; Toki Mabogunje – Deputy President, Lagos Chamber of Commerce / Principal Partner, Toki Mabogunje Consulting; Dada Alamutu – Partner, TAO Alamutu & Partners; and Arinola Adeniyi – Founder & Spa Director, Venivic Health Club & Urban Spa. Others were Jadesola Adedeji – ED, STEM METS Resources; Amara Agbim – Founder/CEO, Nanny Academy; Bike Badeji – Managing Partner, Aragrove LTD/Founder SIGOJA; and Lamiah Momoh – Network marketer/ Executive Coach. Amina Oyagbola- Founder- WISCAR, Fabia Ogunmekan - Executive Secretary, WISCAR.

Representing FCMB were Bukola Smith – ED Business Development, Yetunde Moito- Head SheVentures and Gold Nwoke.
